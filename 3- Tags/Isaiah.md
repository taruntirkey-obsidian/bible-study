An Old Testament prophet.

- Isaiah is considered most major of the major prophets.
- The first among the major prophets.
- Isaiah was specifically sent to the Southern kingdom, Judah.
- Isaiah begins his ministry in around 744 or 740 BC and Judah was destroyed in 722 BC.
- His ministry lasted for 50-60 years.
- His ministry spans four different kings. Maybe even five.
- In scripture, only 4 kings are mentioned, but Jewish legend has it that Manasseh sawed Isaiah in half.
